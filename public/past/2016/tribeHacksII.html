About US

We are hosting our second hackathon ever!
Check out what we are all about.

Create

This will be our second collegiate hackathon at the College of William & Mary. A hackathon is an opportunity for innovative students to come together and bring their software ideas to life.

Dream Big

The perfect opportunity for hackers, from inexperienced to advanced, to think outside the box, push the boundaries of their expectations, and leave knowing they learned and achieved something awesome.

Show Your Passion

We love computers and the things people create with them. And when you spend your weekend slaying your keyboard and crafting the greatest innovation in computing the world has ever seen, people tend to take notice.

Learn

Leave the sustenance to us. We want you to take advantage of every second you can score some sweet prizes. Are you a hacking novice? We'll be hosting introductory tutorials to get you up to speed on the basics of building an app.

Logistics

We'll take care of you, so that you can focus on your hack.

Food

There is no worse feeling than being hungry. We will provide you with full hearty meals as well as good snacks to keep you focused and creative. Catering our event is Zoe's Kitchen, Paul's Deli, and Pita Pit. We'll have good vegetarian options as well. We'll even have Soylent!

Resources & Hardware

First hackathon? Don't worry, we'll have mentors ready to help you out on your hack. The mentors will be able to help you on demand, however we will also be hosting learning seminars. A week from the event we will be releasing the schedule so that you can plan what seminars you'd like to attend. We will have We have Parrot AR 2.0 drones, Myos, Pebbles, Raspberry Pi 3s, Arduinos UNO R3s, and more.

Travel

We have travel reimbursements. Click here to figure out if you are elegible and to register for them. Getting to Williamsburg, VA is easy! We have our very own Amtrak station. If you are interested in taking a bus, Greyhound has a stop at the Amtrak station as well.

FAQ

Schedule?
Click here for a full schedule. Check in will begin at 6pm. Hacking will go until Sunday 9am. Judging will finish by 2pm.
Prizes?
You betcha! We will have over $3000 in prizes. Check them out on our devpost.
Location?
We will be hacking at Small Hall! Check out the Map
Age?
This time around we will not be able to accomodate High School Hackers. We'll be looking to do this next year!
Teams?
You don't need a team to come, we will create a fb group to find teammates closer to the date. Teams can be composed of up to 4 hackers.
Preparations?
Come with ideas, but no code can be written prior to the event. Discuss them with your team and have fun!
